Param-Validator

Livebetrieb

pkwalify -f schema.yml data.yml
  • Gut:
    data.yml#0: valid.
  • Schlecht:
    - [/] Expected required key '-font'
  • Auch schlecht:
    - [/-font] Non-valid data 'HASH(0x5d91d8)', expected a str
  • Nochmals:
    - [/-width] '-12' is too small (< min 0)
